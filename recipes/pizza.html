Homemade Pizza Dough!



Homemade Pizza Dough: The easy way!


Making your own Pizza Dough is not as hard as you would think!

In this Recipe we will show you, how you can do it yourself!


Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 teaspoons of yeast
  • Flour
  • A couple Tablespoons of olive oil
  • Add Salt to your liking
  • 1 Tablespoon of Sugar
  • Cornmeal
  • 1 and 1/3 Cups of 110F Water

Steps

  1. Mix the dough ingredients by hand of with a mixer.
  2. Knead the dough with your hand or a mixer.
  3. Place the dough into a greased mixing bowl and let it rise for 90 minutes.
  4. Release the air bubbles and divide the dough in 2.
  5. Roll dough out into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Top it with your favorite Pizza toppings.
  7. Bake the Pizza at high temperature for 15 minutes

Enjoy!
